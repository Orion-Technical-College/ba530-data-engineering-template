# BA530 Final Course Project: Production Data Platform Handoff

Introduced in Week 7 and developed through sessions 7-1, 7-2, 8-1.

## Purpose

Integrate, demonstrate, challenge, revise, and hand off the cumulative data platform with reproducible evidence and a bounded release decision.

## Cumulative sequence

Carry forward the decisions and evidence from Milestone 3. Do not replace prior conclusions silently; record what changed and why.

## Required deliverables

- complete end-to-end data platform and traceable architecture evidence
- validation_report.md with defects, reconciliation, service results, residual risks, and release decision
- stakeholder_demo.md and handoff_runbook.md with ownership, monitoring, escalation, recovery, cost, and revision triggers

## Evidence standard

Commit executable or inspectable artifacts, captured outputs, tests, and concise decision records. Another reviewer must be able to reproduce the result and determine the condition under which the conclusion should change.

## Submission

Push the completed repository and submit its URL in the matching Canvas assignment. Canvas is the official submission, rubric, feedback, and grade record.

## Final-project standard

This repository is the Final Course Project. It must synthesize the prior three milestones, run from a clean environment, and preserve the final evidence, limitations, and reevaluation plan.
