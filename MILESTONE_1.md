# BA530 Milestone 1: Data Product Charter and Architecture Baseline

Introduced in Week 1 and developed through sessions 1-1, 1-2, 2-1, 2-2.

## Purpose

Define a customer or staff decision, source and consumer contracts, lifecycle, service objectives, architecture options, and an evidence-based baseline.

## Cumulative sequence

This is the first repository in the course sequence. Establish a clean, reproducible foundation for later milestones.

## Required deliverables

- stakeholder_contract.md and data_product_charter.md
- architecture.md with lifecycle states, owners, boundaries, and alternatives
- acceptance_tests.md and architecture_decision_record.md

## Evidence standard

Commit executable or inspectable artifacts, captured outputs, tests, and concise decision records. Another reviewer must be able to reproduce the result and determine the condition under which the conclusion should change.

## Submission

Push the completed repository and submit its URL in the matching Canvas assignment. Canvas is the official submission, rubric, feedback, and grade record.
